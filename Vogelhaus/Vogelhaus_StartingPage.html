Hit the Birdiess!

Start the game. Throw snowballs at birds and hit them (left mouse click). Increase your score.
You'll lose a point if you miss the bird. You have 20 snowballs you can throw.
Feed them to attract them (right/middle mouse click), so it's easier to hit the birds. You only have 3 throws of food.
You can look at the Highscore before and after the game.
